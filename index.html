Staying Safe Online

  • 1 Verify sources before sharing information
  • 2 Avoid clicking unknown links in emails
  • 3 Use strong, unique passwords for each account
  • 4 Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • 5 Be skeptical of urgent requests for money or information
